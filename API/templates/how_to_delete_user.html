How to delete your profile?

How to delete your profile with all data attached (including your business cards and saved business cards)?



To delete your profile, sign into your account in BCard Holder app, press edit icon in right top of screen, than press "Delete profile" button and finnaly press "Yes, delete" button. After following steps you will be redirected to sign in page and your profile with all attached data will be deleted from server
